Information About Our Class

Hello. This website is coming from a high school coding class. We are a class of juniors and seniors in high school. We are located in the south suburbs in Illinois. For the month of February, we are creating a Black History Month website to show different aspects of the Black Lives Matter movement.

Mission Statement

Our goal for this website is to give insight into what the hashtag #blacklivesmatter really means. From topics of how social media affected the movement to how tech jobs affected the movement. Through this website we are hoping to give light to why the Black Lives Matter movement really matter and also why it started.
